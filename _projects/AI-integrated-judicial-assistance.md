## Overview


This project presents a multi-modal polygraph system developed to assist the judicial system in detecting deception and stress during interrogation. The system combines three independent physiological and behavioral detection methods into a unified framework, providing a more comprehensive and reliable analysis than any single method alone. When the readings from lie-detector, ECG sensor and facial-recognitiob model are combined, the system functions as a simple but effective polygraph — cross-referencing physiological signals with behavioral cues to determine whether a subject is exhibiting signs consistent with deception.


## Objectives

- Supports investigators during interrogation
- Provides a more reliable and comprehensive deception analysis
- Ensures proper law-enforcement around criminals

## Technologies

- Lie-detector Circuit
- ECG sensor paired with Arduino UNO
- ML-based facial recognition System
