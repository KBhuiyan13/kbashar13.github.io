I am a Data-Science and AI researcher focusing on data-driven Lightweight, Robust and Explainable AI models that is applicable for multiple real-world domains. I was an undergraduate research assistant at Control Application and Research Center (CARC) where I worked with [Dr. AKM Abdul Malek Azad](https://www.bracu.ac.bd/about/people/akm-abdul-malek-azad-phd). I have received Bachelor’s degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Brac University. I have recently pursued Master's degree in Computer Science from the Royal University of Dhaka(RUD) where my thesis explores compliance assessment in Bangladesh's RMG industry through a mathematical framework. Besides, I have worked with [Dr. Mahdy Rahman Chowdhury](http://ece.northsouth.edu/people/mahdy-rahman-chowdhury/) to develop a hybrid Classical-Quantum Machine Learning Framework for Skin Cancer Detection based on Knowledge Distillation Theory. My previous projects includes satellite training kit and AI-integrated lie-detector polygraph machine. I am currently working in projects related to Quantum-Enhanced Drug Discovery for the Hantavirus. I am interested in research fields like Machine learning, IoT, Cloud Computing, Quantum Computing and satellite communication. I have seven publications across Elsevier, Wiley and IEEE, with a total of 105 citations and an h-index of 3 as of mid-2026.
